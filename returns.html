We want you to shop with confidence. This policy explains how returns, refunds, and buyer protection work on ShareDeal, operated by Selefe Private Limited.

1. 7-day return window

For eligible items, you can request a return within 7 days of receiving your order. The item should be unused and in its original condition and packaging where applicable.

2. What can and can't be returned

  • Returnable: most packaged groceries and household goods that are unused and in original condition.
  • Limited / non-returnable: fresh and perishable items (fruit, vegetables, fish, meat, dairy) cannot be returned for change-of-mind reasons due to their nature — but you are always covered if they arrive damaged, spoiled, wrong, or short. Please report these on receipt.
  • Items marked non-returnable at checkout, and used or opened consumables, are not eligible for change-of-mind returns.

3. How to request a return

  • Open the order in the ShareDeal app and choose Return / Report issue.
  • For damaged, wrong, or short items, attach a photo — it speeds up approval.
  • Our team typically responds within 24 hours and will guide the next step (pickup, drop-off at your Para Neta, or instant refund).

4. Refunds

  • Approved refunds are returned to your ShareDeal wallet by default, or to your original payment method where applicable.
  • Wallet refunds are usually instant; refunds to bKash/Nagad or other methods may take a few business days depending on the provider.
  • Because eligible orders are held in escrow until delivery is confirmed, your money stays protected while an issue is resolved.

5. If your group doesn't fill

If a group order does not reach its target within the time window, the order is automatically cancelled and you are refunded in full — no form, no request needed.

6. Damaged, wrong, or missing items

If something arrives damaged, spoiled, incorrect, or short, report it within the return window (ideally on receipt) with a photo. We'll arrange a refund or replacement at no cost to you.

7. Cancellations

You can cancel an order before it is dispatched. Once an order is out for delivery or collected, the return process above applies.

8. Need help?

Contact support in the app, email sharedealnow@gmail.com, or write to Selefe Private Limited, House 34, Road 4, Block E, Banasree, Rampura, Dhaka-1219.
