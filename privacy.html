ShareDeal is a neighbourhood group-buying platform operated by Selefe Private Limited ("ShareDeal", "we", "us"). This policy explains what information we collect when you use our app and website, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using ShareDeal you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details — your name, mobile number, and (optionally) email, set when you sign up.
  • Order & group information — items you buy, groups you join or create, the neighbours in your group, and your order history.
  • Delivery information — your area/para and pickup point, so a Para Neta can deliver to you. Precise location is only used with your permission.
  • Payment information — for digital payments we receive a confirmation from the payment provider (e.g. bKash/Nagad). We do not store your full card or wallet credentials.
  • Usage & device data — app interactions, device type, and similar diagnostics that help us keep the service reliable and secure.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and run group orders, match you with neighbours, and unlock group pricing.
  • To process orders, payments, refunds, escrow, and delivery through your Para Neta.
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • To operate rewards (coins, bargains, draws) and personalise the deals you see.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and keep the platform safe.
  • To send service messages, and — only with your consent — marketing updates. You can opt out of marketing at any time.

3. When we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share only what is necessary, with:

  • Sellers and Para Netas — the details needed to fulfil and hand over your order.
  • Payment and delivery partners — to process payments and complete delivery.
  • Service providers — vendors that help us run the platform (e.g. hosting, analytics) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities — where required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Payments & escrow

Payments are processed by licensed providers. For eligible orders, funds are held in escrow and released to the seller after delivery is confirmed, which supports our buyer-protection and refund process. We retain transaction records as required for accounting and compliance.

5. Cookies & analytics

Our website may use essential cookies and privacy-respecting analytics to understand usage and improve the experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal and tax obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your choices & rights

  • Access or update your account information in the app.
  • Request a copy or deletion of your personal data, subject to records we must keep by law.
  • Opt out of marketing messages at any time.
  • Withdraw location permission in your device settings.

8. Children

ShareDeal is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. How we protect your data

We use technical and organisational measures — including encrypted transport, access controls, NID-verification of sellers, and escrow — to protect your information. No method is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your data and notify you of significant incidents where required.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version here and update the "last updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
